SmartReg Concept

The Smart Registry concept is an integrated, all-around solution that supports patient-care and clinical data analysis throughout the entire patient journey. The modular structure of the system ensures continuous development and the implementation of a digital research ecosystem.

Data management

Recording, processing and organizing clinical, sampling, diagnostic and therapeutic data, while adhering to high-level security standards, utilizing reliable authentication and authorization.

Integration

Integration capabilities with existing tools and systems (e.g. QCI, MRI) for process automation, as well as data-sharing functionality (file and interface based) for external systems and partners (EESZT, research institutions, foreign partners) ensure interoperability.

Advantages

Smart Patient Registries combine the opportunities inherent in workflow support, process automation and digitalization. With a flexible architecture, each solution is designed to fit customer needs.
